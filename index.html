The Sherlock Principle
Style as observational intelligence
Sherlock Holmes didn't study fashion. He understood that everything a person wears is a data point — wear patterns, fabric quality, fit choices, shoe condition, accessory selections. Style is a language. Learning to dress well and learning to read how others dress are the same skill, developed together. The goal isn't to look good. It's to understand what "looking good" communicates, why it communicates it, and to speak that language with intention. Paul Willstout reads cities; you're learning to read people the same way.
